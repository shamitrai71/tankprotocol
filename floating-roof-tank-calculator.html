Methodology Overview

This calculator implements API MPMS Chapter 19.2 (2012) methodology for estimating volatile organic compound (VOC) emissions from floating roof tanks.

Total emissions are the sum of:

  • LS — Standing Storage Loss
    From seal gaps, roof fittings, roof drains, guide poles. Proportional to vapor pressure and roof area.
  • LW — Working Loss
    From liquid level changes during filling/emptying cycles. Proportional to throughput and vapor-space saturation.
  • LR — Rim Seal Loss Component
    Dominant term in LS. Varies significantly with seal design.
Note: Vapor pressure (P*) should be the true vapor pressure at the mean liquid surface temperature. Use the Reference Data tab for common liquid VP values.
